Deadlock recovery:
	Advantages:
		There is no scheduling of processes. OS allows them to take resources freely.
	Disadvantages:
		Any process can die unexpectedly due to deadlock occurrence.
		There is simultaneously analysis of processes’ workflow
Deadlock prevention:
	Advantages:
		Deadlocks are impossible.
	Disadvantages:
		It’s not effective.
Deadlock avoidance:
	Advantages:
		Deadlocks are rare.
	Disadvantages:
		It’s not always possible to schedule processes for avoiding deadlock
